‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How much screen time is appropriate for my child?

+
For primary school children, we recommend 30-60 minutes of educational screen time per day. Shule AI Plus + games are designed for short, focused learning sessions. Balance digital learning with physical activities, reading, and outdoor play.

How can I monitor my child's progress?

+
Use the Progress Tracking dashboard above to see games played, time spent, and achievements earned. You'll also receive weekly email reports with detailed insights into your child's learning journey and areas of strength.

How do Shule AI Plus + games align with CBC?

+
All our games are designed by Kenyan educators following KICD guidelines. Each game targets specific CBC competencies and learning outcomes for different grade levels, ensuring your child's play time supports their school curriculum.

What if my child struggles with a game?

+
Games are designed with multiple difficulty levels. Encourage your child to start at an easier level and progress gradually. Play together, offer support without giving answers, and celebrate effort over results. Contact our support team for specific guidance.
